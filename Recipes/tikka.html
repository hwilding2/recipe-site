Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

For chicken marinade:

  • chicken thighs
  • plain yogurt
  • spices: minced garlic, ginger, garam masala, turmeric, ground cumin, red chili powder, salt

For sauce:

  • oil (for frying)
  • butter
  • white onion
  • spices: fresh garlic, fresh ginger, garam masala, ground cumin, turmeric, ground coriander, red chili powder, salt
  • tomato puree
  • heavy cream
  • brown sugar
  • water (if needed)
  • fresh cilantro (for garnish)

Directions

  1. Marinade chicken. In a bowl, combine chicken with all of the ingredients for the chicken marinade; let marinate for 10 minutes to an hour.
  2. Cook chicken. Heat oil in large pan and, when sizzling, add chicken pieces. Fry until just browned, about 3 minutes each side (chicken will finish cooking in the sauce).
  3. Fry onions. Melt the butter in the same pan and saute onions for about 3 minutes. Add garlic, ginger, garam masala, cumin, turmeric, and coriander and saute for an additional minute.
  4. Pour in tomato puree, chili powder and salt. Let simmer for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally until sauce thickens.
  5. Stir in cream and sugar. Add chicken back into pan and cook for an additional 8-10 minutes until chicken is cooked through and sauce is thick. Thin with water if needed.
  6. Garnish with cilantro. Serve with rice and garlic naan bread.
