Turkey Bacon Avocado Wrap

Ingredients

  • bacon strips
  • flour tortilla
  • deli turkey
  • avocado
  • lite mayonnaise
  • mustard
  • fried egg (optional)
  • lettuce (optional)

Directions

  1. Cook bacon. Lay the bacon in the cast iron pan so that the bacon isnâ€™t touching, then cook over medium heat until crispy. Lay o paper towel to drain.
  2. Prepare tortilla. Spread lite mayo, mustard, and avocado on tortilla.
  3. Add turkey and bacon. If planning to roll, spread evenly across tortilla. Otherwise, keep in center.
  4. Wrap it up. Fold in the sides of the tortilla and either roll like a pinwheel or wrap like a burrito.
  5. Enjoy! Top with a fried egg if desired.
